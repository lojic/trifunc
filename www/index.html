Topic: Monadic Parser Combinators - J. Vincent Toups

Monadic parser combinators constitute a method of constructing complex parsers out of very simple ones, all without a side-effect in sight. This talk constructs a formalism for building and manipulating parsers in the context of the "parser monad" from the absolute ground up in emacs lisp, discusses monadic binding, does a semi-real-world example, and then hints at constructing alternate parser-monads in terms of monad transformers.
